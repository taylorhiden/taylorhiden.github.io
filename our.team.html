Our Team

Tyler Deaton


President

@tylerdeaton

Tyler Deaton is President of Allegiance Strategies, a DC-based public affairs consulting firm focused on helping clients achieve a freer, safer, and stronger world. Tyler advises the U.S. Committee for Refugees and Immigrants on public policy and conservative engagement. He serves as the Senior Advisor to American Unity Fund and American Unity PAC, working to advance the cause of freedom for LGBT Americans. Tyler is also Senior Advisor to Policy and Taxation Group, Each American Dream, and Family Enterprise USA, advocating on behalf of family businesses to promote free enterprise. Tyler has worked on bipartisan issue advocacy campaigns in the areas of healthcare, immigration, criminal justice, transportation and education. Tyler and his husband James split their time between Concord, N.H. and Washington, D.C.

Nathan Parsons-Schwarz


Executive Vice President

@ParsonsSchwarz

Nathan Parsons-Schwarz is a Partner and Executive Vice President of Allegiance Strategies. He oversees operations, compliance, and finance for Allegiance and several clients. Nathan is a native of Kentucky and graduated from Transylvania University with a degree in political science. He has served as a political appointee in the office of former Kentucky Secretary of State Trey Grayson and has worked as a Republican field operative, including work for the College Republican National Committee and the Republican Parties of Arkansas, Pennsylvania and Virginia.

Keeley Hanlon


Director


Keeley leads Allegiance’s political accountability and public education efforts in the states and at the federal level, including mobilizing center-right constituencies and advancing conservative support across the country for our causes. Before working for Allegiance, Keeley spent three election cycles managing voter outreach, political advertising, and grassroots engagement as part of the political team at the National Federation of Independent Business and went on to manage national field operations at the ONE Campaign. She has assisted with several US Senate and Congressional races and was a part of Wisconsin Governor Scott Walker’s successful effort in the 2012 recall election.

Taylor Hiden


Associate


Taylor manages all of Allegiance's online presence, including websites, social media, graphic design, blogging, email outreach and advertising campaigns. Taylor’s past experience includes work in public relations for Rent the Runway and brand planning for Luckie & Co, an advertising agency based in Birmingham, AL. Taylor holds a B.S. in Business Administration and minor in Mass Communications from Washington and Lee University.

Mike Palicz

Erika Scheffer


Director


Erika oversees advocacy campaigns and policy development across all fifty states. Erika works with elected officials, strategic partners, and major donors to advance causes with conservative support. Erika’s experiences include holding multiple positions over the course of seven and a half years at the National Rifle Association, where she was most recently Deputy Director of Political Affairs at the NRA – Office of Advancement. Previously, Erika also worked for Fairfax County Office of Public Affairs and interned on Capitol Hill for Congressman Bill Shuster (PA-9). Erika earned a Master of Public Policy and a Bachelor of Arts in Government and International Politics from George Mason University.

Torrey Shearer


Senior Director


Named by the D.C. GOP as one of the Most Influential Republicans Under 35, Torrey provides years of public affairs experience lobbying before the U.S. Congress and delivers a comprehensive understanding of the legislative process through analysis of complex and rapidly evolving policy issues. Prior to joining Allegiance, he was a public affairs advisor in the Government Section at Holland & Knight and represented a broad portfolio of corporate, association and tribal government clients. Prior to joining Holland & Knight, Torrey became one of the first members of a team selected by T. Boone Pickens to organize and run a $50 million campaign to turn America’s attention to reducing dependence on foreign oil. Prior to joining the Pickens Plan, he served as a legislative assistant in the federal government affairs office of BNSF Railway Company. He is actively engaged in the national political process and has worked on numerous campaigns at the state and federal level.

Kathleen Wilber


Senior Director


Kathleen Wilber joins the Allegiance team as the Senior Director of Operations and Events. She has spent the last decade and a half working as an event management professional on a wide range of events including non-profit, political and corporate clients. Ms. Wilber found her niche in event management quite by accident. After graduating from Catholic University with a Bachelors in Theater, she took a part time summer job working on the Nations largest fundraising 5-K walk. While meant to be only temporary, she fell in love with the work and ended up turning her passion for creating unique events into a career. Ms. Wilber’s hobbies include working with children, helping to protect animals, especially shelter dogs and cats, and of course, the theater. She currently resides in Arlington, VA.
